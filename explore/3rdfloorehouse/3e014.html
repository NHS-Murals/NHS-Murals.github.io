Untitled (Mountain)

"AIS" (1996)

Located: 3rd Floor, E House-D House Atrium Stairwell

A mural depicting a natural scene dominated by a grey plateau to the right and a forest of evergreens to the left. This is all underneath a large sky with hues of blue, pink, and yellow that takes up about 2/3rds of the mural. Across the sky are the vague shapes of human faces, their eyes all indiscernible and their faces impassive, almost frowning.

Notes: I don't think the faces are supposed to be anyone in particular? Also, please reach out to me if you have any idea who these initials belong to.
