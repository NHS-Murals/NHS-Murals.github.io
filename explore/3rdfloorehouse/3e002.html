Untitled (Sunflower head)

Felix Phillips (2019)

Located: 3rd Floor, E House Staircase

A mural depicting a bust of a humanlike figure. In place of the figure's head is a bunch of yellow sunflowers; they are wearing a white collared shirt with a light blue tattersall pattern on it and a red tie with black teardrop-like shape patterns, and over that is a dark blue suit jacket buttoned just below the breast by a yellow button. A pocket square is tucked into the pocket on their left breast. They are framed by various drops of water, most of them are painted in with blue but the outlying ones appear to just be painted linework.
