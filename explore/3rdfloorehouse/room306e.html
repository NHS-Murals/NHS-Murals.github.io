3RD FLOOR, E HOUSE, ROOM 306E

Most, if not all of these small pieces in Room 306E, the art room, are Senior Squares, a way for departing seniors to make one final mark on this school. Started by a previous art teacher at Norwalk High, Mary Quinlan, who unfortunately passed in 2024, this tradition is still carried on by the current (at the time of this writing) art teacher and a student of Ms. Quinlan, Lauren Delong (who also provided this great background information as well as permission to capture her room, thanks Ms. Delong!).

Due to the sheer amount of these squares, descriptions cannot be provided.
