Untitled (Flower clouds)

Unknown

Located: 3rd Floor, E House-D House Atrium

"An abstract mural of multicolored (particularly red, peach, green, blue, and yellow) flower-shaped blobs against a bright blue background. A white curve is present throughout the whole mural, particularly by its edges, circling around some of the blobs.

Notes: Dubbed this one "Flower clouds" because the blobs look like the flower-like clouds that pattern the sky in the Spongebob cartoon, which are called flower clouds. In other news, the Redudant Department of Redundancy is redudant. Anyways, no signed date but this popped up sometime around late (December) 2024 to early (January) 2025, I can't remember when I first saw it.
