Untitled (Room 306E Pillar)

"Ne" (2022) & Emma Costa (2021)

Located: 3rd Floor, E House, next to the door of Room 306E

A pillar with two murals on it. The top mural is of a tan-skinned face, their eyes and mouth smeared over by some dark multi-colored paint. The bottom is of a typical yellow MTA Metrocard.

Notes: None right now!

Made by Marianne Joy D. Montenegro & Daniel Guo
