Untitled (Beach)

Unknown

Located: 3rd Floor, E House

A mural of a beach. The sand is a rich brown and the foam where the sea and the sand meet appears to be very light. A tall rocky totem-like outcrop frames most of the mural's left, some triangular trees resting above it. In the somewhat close distance, just on the horizon line, is a collection of green mountains with a waterfall in its heart. The orange sky lit by a yellow sun, blue and purple shadowed clouds frame the sky portion of the mural and are across the sky as a whole.

Notes: None right now!
