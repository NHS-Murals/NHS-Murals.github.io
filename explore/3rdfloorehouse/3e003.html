Untitled (Face)

Lara Chard (2000)

Located: 3rd Floor, E House

An abstract mural primarily composed of purple, green, blue, yellow, and some red. The most indentifiable feature of this mural is a face composing a good part of the mural, giving it the apparent context of a portrait. The face is primarly green with swathes of purple about their cheeks, their lips are a grey blue, and their eyes are a dark red with their iris a shocking yellow to contrast. One of their eyes' is pointed downwards while the other forward directly at the viewer.
