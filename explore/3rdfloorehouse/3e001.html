Untitled (Multi-faced)

Kane M., & Tiara T. (2019), Jorgie V. (2018)

Located: 3rd Floor, E House Staircase

A mural depicting a multi-faced blue person's bust wearing a gray jacket, a white shirt, and sunglasses on every one of their face, the middle and most front facing face's sunglasses has a shattered lens. They are framed by a red, blobby frame, the red progressively becoming more darker towards the center, creating the illusion of depth. Their grey jacket drips over this frame.
