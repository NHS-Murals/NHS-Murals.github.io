Untitled (Beautiful)

Samantha Dube (2016) & Bianca Randazzo (2018)

Located: 3rd Floor, E House-D House Atrium

A mural of a pale-skinned person from the neck up against a pink background. The person has a purple flower tucked into their blonde hair, some of which drapes over one of their blue eyes looking directly forward, and red lips. A hand, presumably the person's, holds a blue butterfly. Above them, framed with a mint background and bordered by hot pink circles is the quote in script, Butterflies can't see their wings. They can't see how beautiful they are, but everyone else can. People are like that as well., attributed to Anonymous.

Notes: From what I could find, the quote actually comes from Naya Rivera's[1] book Sorry Not Sorry: Dreams, Mistakes, and Growing Up published 2016.[2] Somewhere in Chapter 2?[3] I don't have a copy of the book so I can't really confirm though.

Shares a wall with Untitled (Art room).
