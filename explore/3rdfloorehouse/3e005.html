Untitled (Beach)

Unknown

Located: 3rd Floor, E House

A small mural in the style of Dr. Seuss depicting a gloved hand tipping a red-and-white striped hat. Just a little beneath it are the words: "Hats off to Mrs. LASKO" and just below those: "1996-2017".

Notes: The Mrs. Lasko the mural is dedicated to refers to Vivian "Viv" Lasko, a retired business technology teacher at Norwalk High School. According to her LinkedIn[1], she helped teach students how to "build, maintain, and repair computers" as well as introduce and teach Cisco Networking Academy to students. Cisco Networking Academy is an American Cisco Systems education program that aims to help course-takers obtain business certifications as well as develop their careers, think something along the lines of IBM's Skillsbuild program. Again from her LinkedIn, Mrs. Lasko started working at NHS on August, 1996 and retired on September, 2017, likely during the beginning of the school year.

Though this mural is unsigned, it is directly next to another Dr. Seuss-inspired mural signed by Felicity & Maria Lagomarsino.
