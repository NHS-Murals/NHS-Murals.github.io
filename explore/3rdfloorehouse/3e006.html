Untitled (Oh, the places you will go!)

Felicity & Maria Lagomarsino (2017)

Located: 3rd Floor, E House

A large mural that references many of Dr. Seuss' works against a simple landscape of a blue sky over green hills. From left to right: Sam-I-Am, a furry Dr. Seuss creature with a yellow body in a red hat, from Green Eggs and Ham carrying a plate of green eggs and ham; a quote in a blocky fonts that reads: Why fit in when you were born to stand out?; a Star-Bellied Sneetch, a yellow bird-like creature with a star on its navel area; an open book with the left page reading: "The more that you read, the more things you will know. The more that you learn, the more places you'll go.", a quote from the book, I Can Read With My Eyes Shut! and the right page being a picture of the Cat in The Hat balancing a teacup, a book, a glass bottle of water, and an fishbowl on his left and an umbrella on his right; a quote from Oh, the Places You'll Go! that reads: "You'll be on your way up! You'll be seeing great sights! You'll join the high fliers Who soar to high heights", which is accompanied by recreation of a drawing from the book that depicts some whimsical airballoons flying under an arch.
