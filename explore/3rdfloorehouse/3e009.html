Untitled (Hope whispers)

Unknown

Located: 3rd Floor, E House, Room 310E

A mural on the door of room 310E. At the top of the door is a mural of a person holding the world (depicted as a blue orb patchworked by paper scraps with text about negative phenomena) over their shoulders as green streaks swirl above them. Below this reads the quote: WHEN THE WORLD SAYS "GIVE UP" HOPE WHISPERS "try 1 more time". The middle half of the door has painted black branches that curl into swirls at their ends.

Notes: I have no idea where this quote came from and all sources claiming it don't seem to be backed up by anything substantial. Please reach out if you have any idea about where this quote from.
