Untitled (Art room)

Carlos Valdovinos, Diego Quiroz, Robertina Lleshdedaj, Edwin Munoz, George Tsaparakis (2009), & "AZ[?]" (2020)

Located: 3rd Floor, E House-D House Atrium

A mural that appears as if there is a hole in a blue wall peeking into an art room. In the foreground, the closest to the hole's opening, are various bulletin paper dispensers and lockers, some closed, some open, behind them. A window with a small panting propped up on it is to the right of these as well. To the left of all of this are some tables with a few students populating them. Further in the background are some computers and various pieces of art on the wall.

Notes: Had to search around for Robertina Lleshdedaj's last name as it was too obscured, found it in the digital archives of our yearbooks eventually.[1] Thank you for the help, Ms. Caiola!

Shares a wall with Untitled (Beautiful).
