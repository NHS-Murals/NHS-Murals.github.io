Untitled (Winnie the Pooh)

Maddy French & Maya Goosmann (2019)

Located: 3rd Floor, D House-C House Hallway

A mural of the Disney rendition of A.A. Milne's Winnie the Pooh, a yellow teddy bear in a red shirt, and Christopher Robin, a pale-skinned boy with brown hair in a bright yellow shirt with a collar, blue shorts, and red shoes; by his raised finger, he appears to be talking to Pooh. The two are sitting on a tree branch against the blue sky, they are framed by the tree's leaves. On those leaves is the quote in silver: "You are Braver than you Believe, Stronger than you Seem, Smarter than you Think."

Notes: Looks to be inspired by this scene in Pooh's Grand Adventure: The Search for Christopher Robin.
