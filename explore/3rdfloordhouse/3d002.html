Untitled (Girl and bear)

Deanna Passarelli (2008)

Located: 3rd Floor, D House-E House Atrium

A small rectangular mural of a purple-haired person with pale skin in a blue hoodie, brown sweats, and orange sneakers looking at a green bear in front of them. The person is holding a black notebook with a yellow bat logo, likely in reference to the DC Comics character Batman. The bear and the person are set against a light blue background patterned with white smears that occassionally curl into swirls at their end. Below this mural is a small black box with the year 2008.

Notes: This mural is above a display case on the wall, hence the size.

Made by Marianne Joy D. Montenegro & Daniel Guo
