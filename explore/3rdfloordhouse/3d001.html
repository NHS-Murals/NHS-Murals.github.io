Untitled (Sun and moon)

Jaime Prohaska & April Harvey (1996)

Located: 3rd Floor, D House-E House Atrium

A large mural depicting a plump-cheeked sun and crescent moon against a swirling background of blue. The sun has particularly blue eyes pointed ahead. The crescent moon has a multicolored rim that outlines its circular shape.
