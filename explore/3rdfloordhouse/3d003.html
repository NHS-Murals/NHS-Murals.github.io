Untitled (Unplugged)

Nora V. & Amanda D. (2016)

Located: 3rd Floor, D House-E House Atrium Stairwell

A mural of a person with a vintage TV in place of their head crouching against a multicolored background. There are wires attached from the TV to the person's back. Below them is the word: UNPLUGGED. Both the person and the word are in black and appear to be spray painted in with a stencil. There are two white patches of paint by the person's left.

Notes: The white patches of paint are covering up some grafitti and probabaly aren't part of the original mural.
