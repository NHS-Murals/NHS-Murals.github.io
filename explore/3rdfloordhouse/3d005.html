Untitled (Careers)

Unknown

Located: 3rd Floor, D House-C House Hallway

A large mural that depicts many people of varying careers on a blue ribbon orginating from an orange-leafed tree in the background. From left to right: a tan-skinned person in firefighter gear depicted hosing down the physical fire alarm over the mural; a pale-skinned green-eyed person with their brown hair in a bun wearing camouflaged fatigues and waving an American flag; a pale-skinned blond-haired blue-eyed person with a curt haircut in a grey suit over a white collared shirt and a red tie holding a briefcase in one hand and a phone up to their ear in the other; a somewhat tan person wearing a sleeveless red jersey with similarly colored shoes and a dark beige pair of shorts ready to launch into a sprint; a pale-skinned person with long blonde hair wearing a purple sleeveless turtleneck sweater and white pants holding a book while appearing as if they are leaning against the tree; a tan-skinned brown-eyed person with their black hair in a bun wearing a lab coat over a blue collared shirt and a grey pencil skirt with a red stethoscope around their neck as well as holding a grey sheet; a brown-skinned brown-eyed person with red and pink hair wearing a grey hoodie with a hood over their head and matching pants, they appear to be leaning but a light sketch of their other leg shows that they were intended to be running; and a pale-skinned brown-haired brown-eyed person with shaggy hair and in casual attire sitting on a stool and strumming a guitar.

Notes: None right now!
